Careers at Resyvex

Build technology education. Mentor engineers. Make an impact.

Why Join Resyvex

We're a mission-driven team building practical technology education for engineers and students. If you're passionate about mentorship, technology, and learning, Resyvex is where you belong.

Meaningful Work

Impact thousands of students by teaching skills that shape careers. See direct results of your mentorship.

Tech + Education

Work at the intersection of cutting-edge technology and human development. Both matter equally.

Growth Mindset

We invest in our team. Learn new skills, grow as a mentor, and advance your career with us.

Supportive Culture

Collaborative team where clarity, honesty, and respect matter. No office politics, just good work.

Student-First Values

Every decision centers on what's best for students and learning. Work with purpose.

Flexibility

Remote options, flexible schedules for the right roles. We trust our team to deliver.

Our Culture

How We Work

  • Collaborative: Teams work together, support each other, and celebrate wins together
  • Student-focused: Every decision asks: "Is this good for students?"
  • Practical: We prefer doing over talking. Results matter
  • Learning-oriented: We grow. Mistakes are learning, not failures
  • Clear & Honest: We communicate directly and respectfully, no hidden agendas
  • Quality-first: We don't compromise on work quality or student experience

Open Roles

Current opportunities at Resyvex. We're always looking for passionate individuals to join our mission.

🔹 Project Engineer (Electronics / IoT / Embedded)

Design and develop student projects (Arduino, ESP32, IoT, ML), circuit design and PCB development, testing and debugging prototypes, support students during project explanation.

🔹 Project Mentor / Trainer

Guide students in understanding concepts, help in report preparation and viva training, provide hands-on training sessions.

🔹 Technical Content Developer

Prepare project documentation, create PPTs, reports, and manuals, develop training materials.

🔹 Student Coordinator

Handle student communication, manage project timelines and delivery, coordinate between students and engineers.

🔹 Embedded Systems Engineer

Work on microcontrollers (Arduino, ESP32, STM32), develop firmware and hardware integration, real-time system development.

🔹 IoT Developer

Build cloud-connected devices, work with apps and dashboards, data monitoring and automation.

🔹 PCB Design Engineer

Design circuits using KiCad/Altium, schematic and layout design, prototype manufacturing support.

🔹 Software Developer

Develop web/mobile apps for IoT control, backend integration and APIs.

🔹 R&D Engineer

Work on innovative product ideas, research emerging technologies, prototype new concepts.

🔹 Technical Sales Engineer

Communicate with clients, understand requirements, provide technical solutions.

🔹 Technical Trainer

Teach Embedded Systems, IoT, PCB Design, conduct workshops and bootcamps, provide hands-on training.

🔹 Course Developer

Design structured learning programs, create curriculum and modules, develop assignments and projects.

🔹 Lab Instructor

Maintain lab equipment, assist students during practical sessions.

🔹 Career Counselor

Guide students on career paths, help with placements and resumes.
