Services by Resyvex

Flexible service solutions for students, startups, and organizations needing practical technology delivery, training, and career-ready support.

What We Deliver

Resyvex offers a comprehensive range of services designed to bridge the gap between learning and real-world application. From hands-on training to full project delivery, we support students, startups, and organizations at every stage.

Training & Workshops

Structured courses and interactive workshops in PCB design, embedded systems, robotics, IoT, and more. Hands-on learning that builds practical skills and confidence.

Projects & Consulting

End-to-end project delivery and expert consulting for embedded systems, electronics, and product development. We guide from concept to completion.

3D Printing & Prototyping

Rapid prototyping services using advanced 3D printing technology. Turn ideas into physical models quickly and cost-effectively.

Software/Web Services

Custom software development, web applications, and digital solutions tailored to your needs. From mobile apps to enterprise systems.

Student Support & Career Guidance

Personalized mentoring, portfolio reviews, internship opportunities, and career coaching to help students transition from learning to earning.
