Franchise Opportunity

Scale practical tech education to your city. Partner with Resyvex to build a trusted learning hub.

What is Resyvex Franchise

The Resyvex franchise is a partnership opportunity for educators, entrepreneurs, and training institutes to bring our practical tech learning and project support model to new locations. You get a proven curriculum, brand trust, and operational support. Your community gets access to quality engineering education.

Why Partner with Resyvex

Proven Model

We've delivered courses to 1,200+ students and completed 320+ projects. You get a tested framework, not an experiment.

Brand Trust

Resyvex is recognized for quality and integrity. Launch with established brand credibility in your market.

Complete Curriculum

Ready-to-deliver courses in electronics, embedded systems, PCB design, and career guidance. No building from scratch.

Operational Support

Training for your team, guidance on student recruitment, lab setup, and day-to-day operations.

Marketing Resources

Branded materials, digital assets, and strategies to reach students and institutions in your area.

Ongoing Mentorship

Regular coaching, curriculum updates, quality checks, and access to the Resyvex network.

Who Can Apply

We're looking for passionate educators and entrepreneurs who share our vision of practical tech education. Ideal candidates include:

  • Technical educators & trainers with experience in STEM or engineering education
  • Workshop owners & lab managers looking to expand their offerings
  • Training institutes wanting to add tech courses to your curriculum
  • Entrepreneurs passionate about education and tech
  • Engineers & professionals wanting to give back through education
  • Career counselors wanting to offer students practical skills

Key traits we look for: Commitment to quality, student-first mindset, basic tech knowledge, local network, and willingness to follow the Resyvex model while adapting to your context.

Franchise Partnership Process

Step 1: Application
Submit your details and tell us about your background, location, and vision. We review fit and market potential.
Step 2: Initial Conversation
We schedule a call to discuss your goals, answer questions, and understand your market and resources.
Step 3: Evaluation & Proposal
Based on your profile, we create a customized proposal with investment, timeline, and support plan.
Step 4: Onboarding
Once agreed, you get trainer certification, curriculum materials, and setup guidance to launch your center.
Step 5: Soft Launch
Start with pilot courses, gather feedback, and optimize your operations with our support.
Step 6: Full Launch
Launch full course offerings, run marketing campaigns, and scale with ongoing Resyvex support.

Investment & Terms

Franchise investment varies based on your location, scale, and local conditions. Typical considerations include:

Initial Investment

Covers curriculum, training, brand setup, and initial marketing. Customized based on your readiness and market.

Operational Costs

Lab space, equipment, staff, utilities, and marketing. You maintain operational independence and manage locally.

Revenue Model

You keep revenue from course fees. Monthly or annual support fee to Resyvex (percentage of revenue or fixed).

Timeline to Profitability

Typically 12-18 months depending on execution and market. We support you to achieve growth milestones.

Note: We'll discuss detailed financial terms, territory rights, and contractual obligations during the evaluation phase. Our goal is a win-win partnership where you succeed.
