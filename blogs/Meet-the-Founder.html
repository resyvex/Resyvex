Meet the Founder: Cheraladhan Krish and the Vision of Resyvex

Published on September 24, 2025 by Cheraladhan Krish

Every big idea starts with a spark. For Cheraladhan Krish, that spark was a deep passion for technology, innovation, and problem-solving. With years of experience in electronics, IoT projects, design, and hands-on learning, Cheraladhan founded Resyvex to create a platform where ideas can grow, take shape, and inspire.

The Founder’s Vision

Cheraladhan believes that learning should not stop at theory it should be experienced, built, and tested. Many students and young innovators have brilliant ideas but lack the right guidance, tools, or resources to bring them to life. Resyvex was born to bridge this gap to provide support, resources, and services that make innovation accessible to everyone.

What Resyvex Will Do

Resyvex is not just a company it’s a hub for innovation and creativity. Here’s what we offer:

Custom Projects for Students

  • School-level science projects, mini projects, and final-year engineering projects.
  • Guidance from concept to execution.
  • Hands-on learning support so students understand their projects deeply.

3D Printing Services

  • High-quality 3D printing for prototypes, models, and product parts.
  • Design-to-print services for students, startups, and hobbyists.
  • Turning imagination into tangible reality.

Design Services

  • CAD modeling, electronics design, PCB development, and prototyping.
  • End-to-end design support for personal, academic, or professional use.
  • Helping ideas look and function like real-world products.

Electronics Store

  • A one-stop shop for essential components, sensors, and modules.
  • Reliable parts for makers, students, and engineers.
  • Easy access to everything needed for projects and experiments.

Workshops & Training

  • Hands-on learning sessions in electronics, IoT, robotics, and 3D printing.
  • Practical, project-based workshops for schools, colleges, and communities.
  • Inspiring the next generation of innovators through knowledge sharing.

The Future of Resyvex

Resyvex is committed to building a strong innovation ecosystem. By empowering students, creators, and startups with the right tools and knowledge, we aim to become a trusted partner in education, technology, and design.

A Message from the Founder

“Resyvex is more than a company it’s a movement to make technology simple, accessible, and inspiring. Whether you are a student, a hobbyist, or an innovator, we are here to help you bring your ideas to life.”
— Cheraladhan Krish, Founder of Resyvex
