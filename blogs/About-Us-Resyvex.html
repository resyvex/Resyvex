About Us - Resyvex

Published on November 12, 2025 by Cheraladhan Krish

About Resyvex

Resyvex is a multi-domain innovation and technology platform designed to empower students, creators, and industries through hands-on learning, automation, and design solutions. It serves as a bridge between academics and real-world applications, offering practical project development and industry-driven services.

Founded and led by Cheraladhan Krish, managed by Lokesh Velayudham (Project Manager) Resyvex is built on the belief that learning by doing creates innovators. The platform unites education, research, and technology under one vision to help learners and professionals bring their ideas to life.

Meaning / Motto

Resyvex stands for Research Your Vision Experiments — a name that reflects our core belief: every innovation begins with a vision and continuous experimentation.

What We Offer

Resyvex provides complete, end-to-end support for students, startups, and industries through the following services:

  • ✅ End-to-end support for capstone and mini-projects
  • ✅ IoT, Embedded Systems, and Machine Learning project development guidance
  • ✅ Custom electronics design (PCB, circuits, IoT prototypes)
  • ✅ Research paper writing and publication guidance
  • ✅ Internships and skill-development workshops
  • ✅ Industrial automation and control systems
  • ✅ 3D design and printing for prototypes
  • ✅ Electronics components and spares supply for students and industries

Resyvex offers bilingual mentoring in Tamil and English, making it accessible and practical for learners across Tamil Nadu and India.

Our Divisions

🔹 Resyvex Projects

Dedicated to college and school students for academic and innovation-based projects. We guide students through IoT, Embedded, Machine Learning, and Electronics concepts with a focus on real-world application and practical understanding.

🔹 Resyvex Industrial Automation Pvt. Ltd.

Provides industrial-level automation and control system solutions, including PLC-based control, IoT monitoring, and process optimization for industrial setups.

🔹 Resyvex Workshops

Organizes hands-on workshops in IoT, Arduino, PCB Design, 3D Printing, and Embedded Systems — taught in Tamil and English to simplify learning for all students.

🔹 Resyvex 3D Design

Specializes in 3D modeling and printing for prototypes and industrial components, turning digital concepts into physical products with precision.

🔹 Resyvex Electronics Components & Spares

Supplies high-quality electronic components, sensors, and IoT modules for students, hobbyists, and industries. Ensures the availability of trusted components for every project need.

Our Vision

To inspire innovation by connecting students, creators, and industries through technology, education, and automation — nurturing a new generation of inventors and problem-solvers.

Our Mission

To make project development, research, and industrial solutions affordable, accessible, and hands-on for every learner and professional.
