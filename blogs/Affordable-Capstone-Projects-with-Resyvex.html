Affordable Capstone Projects with Resyvex

Published on September 28, 2025 by Cheraladhan Krish

Innovation Made Simple: Affordable Capstone Projects by Resyvex

When it comes to engineering and technology education, one of the most exciting milestones for students is their capstone project. It’s the moment where theory meets practice, and ideas are transformed into working models. However, many students struggle to find projects that are affordable, reliable, and impactful. That’s where Resyvex comes in.

We’re proud to introduce our “Just Normal Capstone Project”, priced at only ₹1400. This project is designed to give students a budget-friendly yet practical solution that not only fulfills academic requirements but also helps them gain real-world hands-on experience.

Why Capstone Projects Matter

A capstone project is more than just another assignment—it’s a way to:

  • Demonstrate technical knowledge learned throughout the course.
  • Apply problem-solving skills to practical challenges.
  • Showcase innovation to peers, faculty, and future employers.
  • Boost confidence by creating something that works in the real world.

Unfortunately, many students either overspend on expensive kits or end up with projects that don’t function as expected. Resyvex solves this problem by providing tested, affordable, and ready-to-use project solutions.

The Capstone Project – ₹1400

Our featured project is a simple, effective, and easy-to-use model that blends electronics and IoT fundamentals. With built-in components like:

  • ESP32 microcontroller
  • Ultrasonic sensor
  • OLED display
  • Wi-Fi connectivity
    •

This project gives students exposure to core concepts such as sensor integration, microcontroller programming, and IoT communication. It’s affordable, practical, and comes with the necessary support to ensure you can present it with confidence.

Behind Resyvex is Cheraladhan Krish, an innovator passionate about making technology accessible for everyone. His journey began with a deep curiosity for electronics and a vision to create a platform where students and creators could learn, build, and innovate without barriers.

Cheraladhan strongly believes that education should not stop at theory. Instead, students should be able to experiment, build prototypes, and experience innovation firsthand. This belief inspired him to establish Resyvex, a space where learning becomes fun, practical, and future-ready.

What Resyvex Offers

Resyvex is more than just a project provider—we are a hub of innovation and learning. Our services include:

🔹 Custom Academic Projects – from mini-projects to complex final-year designs.

🔹 3D Printing Services – transforming CAD designs into working prototypes.

🔹 Design Support – CAD modeling, PCB design, and product prototyping.

🔹 Electronics Store – components, sensors, and modules all under one roof.

🔹 Workshops & Training – hands-on sessions in IoT, robotics, and electronics.

We don’t just hand over projects—we guide, mentor, and ensure that every student understands the fundamentals behind their model.
